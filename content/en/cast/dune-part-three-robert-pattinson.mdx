<Section icon={Target} title="Plot and Dune Messiah Storyline">

The plot of **Dune Part Three** picks up several years after the conclusion of the second film. It adapts Frank Herbert’s *Dune Messiah*, a novel that deconstructs the "hero's journey" established in the first book. Paul Atreides is now the most powerful man in the universe, but his power has come at a terrible cost: a bloody jihad carried out in his name across the stars.

The narrative focuses on the following core pillars:
- **The Conspiracy**: The Tleilaxu, the Bene Gesserit, and the Spacing Guild unite to destroy Paul's influence.
- **The Ghola**: The return of Duncan Idaho (Jason Momoa) as a "ghola" named Hayt, a resurrected being with no memory of his past life.
- **The Heir**: The tension surrounding Paul's lack of a biological heir and the political maneuvering of Princess Irulan.
