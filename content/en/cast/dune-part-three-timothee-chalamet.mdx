The story of the third film adapts Frank Herbert's 1969 sequel novel, *Dune Messiah*. This chapter serves as a cautionary tale about the dangers of hero worship, showing how Paul's victory at the end of *Dune: Part Two* unleashed a devastating holy war across the cosmos. 

### The Core Conflict: Paul vs. Chani

At the end of the second film, Paul chose a political marriage with Princess Irulan (Florence Pugh) to secure the throne, prompting Chani to ride away into the deep desert. The third film picks up with this emotional damage. The official trailer highlights intense confrontations where Chani challenges Paul's imperial authority and his reliance on religious fanaticism.
