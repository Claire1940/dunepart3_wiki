<Section icon={Map} title="The Origins of Dune Part Three Lady Jessica">

The character of **dune part three lady jessica** remains one of the most complex figures in Frank Herbert's sci-fi epic. Born in 10154 AG on Wallach IX, Jessica was the product of a highly controlled Bene Gesserit breeding program. Her biological parentage was kept secret from her, a standard protocol of the Sisterhood to prevent personal attachments from interfering with their grand design. 

Her lineage connects two of the most powerful and warring houses in the Imperium. This secret heritage heavily influences her motivations, her physical attributes, and the tragic trajectory of her family line.

| Children | Paul Atreides, Alia Atreides |

<Callout type="warning" title="The Breeding Plan Failure">
The Bene Gesserit originally ordered Jessica to produce an Atreides daughter. This daughter was meant to wed Feyd-Rautha Harkonnen to unite the houses and birth the Kwisatz Haderach. Out of love for Duke Leto, Jessica chose to conceive a son instead, disrupting centuries of careful planning.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="The Jessica Crime and Its Galactic Legacy">

In the wider lore of the Dune universe, Jessica's actions are studied for millennia. Her choice to prioritize her love for Duke Leto over the direct commands of the Bene Gesserit Sisterhood is officially designated as "The Jessica Crime." This decision ultimately led to the rise of Paul Muad'Dib, the Fremen Jihad, and the eventual reign of the God Emperor Leto II.
