<Section icon={Swords} title="The Return of Duncan Idaho as Hayt">

One of the most anticipated elements of **dune part three hayt** is the return of Jason Momoa. While Duncan Idaho died heroically defending Paul and Jessica in the first film, the sequel introduces the concept of a "ghola"—a biological clone created by the Bene Tleilax. In the novel *Dune Messiah*, this ghola is named Hayt. He is gifted to Paul as a "present" by his enemies, serving as both a philosopher-mentor and a potential psychological weapon.

Hayt is not merely a copy of Duncan; he is a Mentat and a Zensunni philosopher, possessing skills the original Duncan never had. This creates a profound emotional conflict for Paul, who sees the face of his oldest friend but knows the man before him is a product of Tleilaxu laboratories. The film explores whether the original memories of Duncan Idaho can ever be recovered or if Hayt is a permanent replacement.

The narrative of **dune part three hayt** picks up twelve years after Paul Atreides ascended to the throne. The "Holy War" has spread across the universe, resulting in billions of deaths.
