In the narrative timeline of *Dune Messiah*, Paul Atreides has ruled as Emperor of the Known Universe for over a decade. His holy war, or Jihad, has swept across the cosmos, leaving billions dead and cementing his status as a reluctant messiah. This concentration of absolute power breeds a deadly alliance among the factions he displaced or bypassed.

The conspiracy against Paul is unique because it does not rely on direct military might. Instead, it utilizes psychological warfare, bio-engineering, and political manipulation. **Dune part three scytale** serves as the operational hand of this alliance.

<Section icon={Target} title="How Scytale Manipulates the Atreides Empire">

To break Paul Atreides, Scytale targets the Emperor's deepest emotional vulnerabilities: his grief over lost loved ones and his struggle with prescience.
