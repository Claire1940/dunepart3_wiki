<Section icon={Target} title="New Threats: Scytale and the Tleilaxu">

A major shift in the **dune part three plot** is the introduction of the Tleilaxu, a secretive society of genetic engineers. Their representative, Scytale, played by Robert Pattinson, serves as the primary antagonist. Unlike the brute force of the Harkonnens, the Tleilaxu use biological manipulation, psychological warfare, and shapeshifting "Face Dancers" to undermine their enemies.

**Key Antagonists and Their Roles:**
- **Scytale**: A Face Dancer who leads the conspiracy against Paul. He offers Paul the chance to "resurrect" Chani in exchange for his empire.
- **Reverend Mother Mohiam**: Seeking to regain control of the Atreides genetics for the Bene Gesserit.
- **Edric**: A Guild Navigator whose prescience hides the conspiracy from Paul's own prophetic visions.
