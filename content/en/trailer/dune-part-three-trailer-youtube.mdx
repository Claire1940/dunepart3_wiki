<Section icon={Target} title="Plot and Dune Messiah Story Guide">

The narrative of the third film is based on *Dune Messiah*, the second book in Frank Herbert's series. It takes place several years after the events of the second film. The story is a subversion of the "Hero's Journey," showing the dangers of following a charismatic leader.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Holy War">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Conspiracy">
    The Bene Gesserit, the Spacing Guild, and the Tleilaxu form a secret alliance to dethrone Paul. They use the resurrected Duncan Idaho as a psychological weapon.

  <Step num="3" title="The Personal Cost">
