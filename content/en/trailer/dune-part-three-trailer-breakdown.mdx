<Section icon={Target} title="Dune Messiah Plot Adaptations">

This film serves as a direct adaptation of Frank Herbert's 1969 novel, *Dune Messiah*. This segment of the story is intentionally darker, serving as a deconstruction of the classic hero's journey established in the first two films.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Holy War">
    Following the events of Part Two, Paul's Fremen legions have launched a jihad across the universe, killing billions in his name.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Conspiracy">
    The Bene Gesserit, the Spacing Guild, and the Tleilaxu form a secret alliance to strip Paul of his absolute power.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Ghola Gift">
    The conspirators present Paul with a clone (Ghola) of his deceased friend Duncan Idaho, designed to trigger psychological instability.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Ultimate Sacrifice">
    Paul must choose between maintaining his absolute grip on the empire or saving his family from an inevitable tragedy.
  </Step>
</Steps>
